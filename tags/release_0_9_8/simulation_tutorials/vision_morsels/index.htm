USIP Online Simulation Creation Vision Morsels

     

 

Vision Morsels

In a nutshell, this project is to create an 'online simulation wizard' that will enable subject matter experts to create 'online simulations.' But a lot is implied in that one short sentence. So explanations of what this wizard does, and what we envision it will be able to do someday, have been broken down into bite-sized chunks. Each of these can be accessed below.

feedback is always welcome

Morsel 0: The Big "Why?"
 
  Before we chew on these morsels, a few words of justification are merited about why we're creating this tool. Online simulations are the way of the future, and many new tools are coming online every day. Why is the United States Institute of Peace investing in the creation of this tool?

Morsel 3: The Point of View Example
 
 

Seeing something from another's point of view can be the first step of conducting meaningful negotiations.

 
Morsel 4: The P.O.V. Ultimatum
  Illustration
  In this morsel we build on the previous morsel, but now add the ability of the players to play the ultimatum game.
Morsel 5: Exponential Growth
  Illustration
  Some problems seem to grow slowly, but then explode onto the scene. The human mind does not easily deal with exponential growth. This morsel takes lessons from biology to help teach players the wisdom of prevention: A problem that is small, but growing exponentially, may be easy to deal with today and impossible to deal with tomorrow.
Morsel 6: Dealing with Budgets
  Illustration
  If you want to know what is important to someone, see how they spend their money. In this morsel we allow players to prioritize their concerns by being able to allocate their budgets against them.
Morsel 7: A 'Simulation Section' is just a web page
  Illustration
  In this morsel we describe how different web tools can be incorporated into a simulation. A 'simulation section' page is just a web page somewhere. We are working on a well defined API to allow other tools to speak to simulations created with the USIP OSP.
Morsel 8: Macrofiscal Policy
  Illustration
  In this morsel we describe how underlying economic models can be 'plugged in' to the tool to allow us to turn our simulations into economic simulators.
